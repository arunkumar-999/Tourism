KANYAKUMARI TOURIST PLACES

Situated on a rocky island about 500 meters offshore, the Vivekananda Rock Memorial is a sacred monument dedicated to Swami Vivekananda, the renowned Hindu monk and philosopher. Visitors can reach the memorial by ferry and explore the meditation hall and Vivekananda Mandapam, which offers panoramic views of the surrounding seas.
